Perspective and Proportion

March 16

Subject: A class in two parts: first, Professor Gifford's lecture on the history of perspective, then a day outside tracing architecture in proportion on mirrors or glass.

Notes: Tracing on the transparent surfaces made an interesting case for just how extreme perspective effects can be naturally, though the mind normalizes our perception of it. Due to the nature of the mirror I was drawing on, the drawing proved a little difficult to document!
