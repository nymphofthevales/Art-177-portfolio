The Lightness of Stone

Flow Visualization

Project: An illustration to visualize the flow of some system, liquid, gas, etc...

Notes: I was lost on what to do for this project for a long time. Then, one afternoon I was doodling topographical patterns for a tattoo I would like to get, and the flowing lines spoke to me.

    The fluid system that my project tackles is that of stone, a metamorphosis and realization of stone as something light and fluid. This project includes a couple drawings, a looping animation, and a short essay fleshing out the subject and connecting a few different ideas.

If the embeded pdf doesn't show in your browser, you can view the pdf in a new tab here.
